# Universal Credit Hub — 90-Second Demo Script

> Use this script for: investor calls, sales demos, regulator briefings, conference lightning talks, and the LinkedIn launch video. Adjust pacing to your audience.

**Demo URL:** https://universalcredithub.replit.app
**Demo credentials:** `johndoe` / `SecuredCreditor2026!`

---

## 0:00 — 0:10 | The hook (10s)

> "300 million African adults have no formal credit history. Lenders can't see them, so they don't get loans. We fixed that."

**Screen:** Universal Credit Hub homepage at `/`. Hover over the three product cards.

---

## 0:10 — 0:25 | The platform (15s)

> "Universal Credit Hub is three systems in one platform — a pan-African credit bureau, a movable-asset collateral registry, and Loto Fiscal, which turns verified VAT receipts into a credit profile. They're wired together by a consent-bounded data bridge."

**Screen:** Click the dashboard tile. Land on the animated investor hero showing 14.7M+ borrowers ticking up, six regulator badges (BoG, CBN, BCEAO, BEAC, CBK, BSL).

---

## 0:25 — 0:40 | The flagship moment — fiscal receipts → credit (15s)

> "Watch this. A merchant in Côte d'Ivoire rings sales on a Loto-registered POS. Every receipt is verified by the tax authority. After six months, the merchant flips one toggle…"

**Screen:** Navigate to `/loto-workspace` → "Build Credit Profile" tab. Click the opt-in toggle. The consent record appears.

---

## 0:40 — 1:00 | The lender side (20s)

> "…and now a lender opening that merchant's credit report sees a brand-new section: VAT Activity Score, monthly turnover, growth trend, reason codes. The merchant just went from invisible to fundable."

**Screen:** Search for the merchant in `/search`. Open the credit report. Scroll to the VAT Activity section. Highlight the gateway-provenance badge.

---

## 1:00 — 1:15 | The trust layer (15s)

> "Every cross-product access goes through one auditable bridge. Consent ID, purpose, expiry, all logged. Anchored to a public blockchain every six hours. Regulators can watch in real time."

**Screen:** Open `/audit` → filter "Cross-product accesses". Show a row with consent ID + purpose. Then open `/financial-inclusion` → live ticker.

---

## 1:15 — 1:30 | The close (15s)

> "54 countries. Seven languages. Four legal regimes. One platform. We're not trying to out-data TransUnion in the U.S. — we're building what they would build if they started today, for Africa, with consent baked in. Universal Credit Hub. Let's get every African a credit history they own."

**Screen:** Cut to the master landing page hero. End on the Universal Credit Hub logo.

## Filming tips

- **Record in 1920×1080** at 60fps. Browser at 110% zoom.
- **Voice:** confident, conversational, not salesy. Pauses are powerful.
- **Cursor:** highlight clicks visually (Loom does this automatically). Move slowly.
- **Music:** light, upbeat, instrumental. Drop the music for the closing line.
- **Captions:** burn in subtitles — most LinkedIn views are muted.
- **Length variants to cut:**
  - 90s — full demo (this script)
  - 60s — drop section 4 (cross-product gateway)
  - 30s — keep only sections 1, 3, and the close
