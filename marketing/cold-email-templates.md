## Sending tips

- **Send Tuesday–Thursday, 7–9 AM** local recipient time. Avoid Mondays and Fridays.
- **One ask per email.** Demo, intro call, exclusive — pick one.
- **Personalize the first line.** If you can't, don't send it.
- **Follow up after 4 business days** with a one-line bump: "Bumping this up — happy to send the 90-second demo regardless." Then again at day 10. Then drop it.
- **Track opens** with a simple link UTM, not pixel trackers (they trip spam filters).
