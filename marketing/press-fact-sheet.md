## One-line description

**Universal Credit Hub** is the consent-first credit infrastructure for Africa — a single platform combining a pan-African credit bureau, a movable-asset collateral registry, and a verified VAT-receipt system (Loto Fiscal) — wired together by a consent-bounded data bridge so previously invisible merchants can unlock real credit.

## Three-line description

Universal Credit Hub combines three systems that have never been on one platform: a pan-African credit bureau (54 countries), a movable-asset collateral registry (OHADA, UCC-9, Common, Civil), and Loto Fiscal — a fiscal-receipt platform whose verified VAT data doubles as alternative-data credit scoring for thin-file merchants.

Every cross-product data flow runs through one auditable consent bridge: explicit purpose, expiry, instant revocation, and a blockchain-anchored audit chain that regulators can watch in real time.

The result: lenders see deeper applicant pictures, regulators get a modern supervisory window, and the 300+ million African adults without traditional credit files get a path to a credit history they own.

## Why now

1. **Mobile money, POS, and fiscal-receipt platforms** have created enough digital paper trail for alternative-data scoring to actually work in Africa.
2. **OHADA's 2010 Uniform Act on Securities** modernised collateral law across 17 countries; the registries to operationalise it are still mostly paper.
3. **GDPR-aligned data-protection laws** are now in force across most African economies (Ghana DPA, Nigeria NDPR, Kenya DPA, etc.) — consent-first architecture is a regulatory requirement, not a nice-to-have.
4. **Cross-border lending and fintech** keep growing; regulators need a real-time supervisory window the legacy bureaus can't provide.

## Product details

### Credit Bureau
Pan-African consumer & business credit reporting. AI-driven portfolio intelligence. Built-in regulator exports for BoG, CBN, BCEAO, BEAC, CBK, BSL. Real-time scoring (300–850), reason codes, dispute workflow, court judgments, dishonoured cheques, telco lending integration.

### Collateral Registry
Movable-asset lien registration adapted to four legal regimes. Supports OHADA Uniform Act on Securities, UCC Article 9, English Common Law and French Civil Law. PMSI tracking, certificate generation, public search, lender notice.

### Loto Fiscal
Verified VAT-receipt platform. Each receipt is signed by the tax authority. Merchant opt-in turns months of receipts into a 300–850 VAT Activity Score with reason codes (STRONG_RECEIPT_FREQUENCY, GROWING_TURNOVER, RECENT_DROP_IN_VOLUME, NEW_RECEIPT_HISTORY, THIN_RECEIPT_FILE) — usable directly by credit lenders through the consent bridge.
