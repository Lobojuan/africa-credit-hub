# Launch Day Checklist — Africa Credit Hub

> Run through this the morning of your public launch. Aim to publish between **9–10 AM West Africa Time** so you catch both Africa and Europe in their morning windows.

---

## T-7 days

- [ ] Record the 90-second demo video (see `demo-script.md`)
- [ ] Cut three video variants: 90s landscape, 60s landscape, 30s vertical
- [ ] Add captions in English; arrange French dub or captions
- [ ] Upload videos to YouTube as unlisted; copy the share URL into the press kit
- [ ] Brief your investors / advisors so they can amplify
- [ ] Schedule a soft-pitch call with one friendly journalist (embargo until launch day)
- [ ] Confirm the deployed app loads, demo creds work, all three product surfaces look polished

## T-3 days

- [ ] Write the launch LinkedIn post using `linkedin-launch.md` Post 1
- [ ] Schedule it for 9 AM launch day (or post live)
- [ ] Draft Posts 2–6 in your scheduler so you don't have to think launch-week
- [ ] Email the 6 cold-outreach templates from `cold-email-templates.md` to a personalized list of 30 contacts (5 per template)
- [ ] Update LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and personal website bios to mention Africa Credit Hub
- [ ] Add Open Graph image to homepage so link previews look professional

## T-1 day

- [ ] Final smoke test: visit `/`, `/for-lenders`, `/for-regulators`, `/press`, `/financial-inclusion` on desktop and mobile
- [ ] Test the demo login with a brand-new browser session
- [ ] Confirm all language toggles work (especially French)
- [ ] Confirm the 90-second demo video plays muted with captions readable
- [ ] Charge phone, set "do not disturb" except for messages

## Launch day (T-0)

**07:30 AM**
- [ ] Post the LinkedIn launch (Post 1) with the demo video attached
- [ ] Cross-post to X (Twitter) — same caption, shorter
- [ ] Cross-post to your personal LinkedIn if the company page is brand-new

**09:00–11:00 AM**
- [ ] Send the journalist embargo-lift email
- [ ] Send the founder-to-founder warm intro emails (Template 6)
- [ ] Reply to every comment on the launch post within 10 minutes
- [ ] DM five investors with the deck and the demo URL

**12:00 PM**
- [ ] Lunch. Step away from the screen for 30 minutes. Yes, really.

**13:00–17:00 PM**
- [ ] Send cold templates 1, 2, 3 to your warm list
- [ ] Reply to comments and messages
- [ ] Take a screenshot of the post engagement at 4 hours and at end of day for your records

**Evening**
- [ ] Personally text or call 5 mentors / champions and thank them for sharing
- [ ] Write a 5-line journal entry: what worked, what didn't, what to change tomorrow

## What "going viral" actually looks like (so you don't panic)

- **First 30 minutes:** crickets. Normal.
- **30 min – 4 hrs:** likes from your network. Comments start. Reply to every one.
- **4–24 hrs:** if it's spreading, you'll see strangers in the comments. That's the algorithm picking it up.
- **24–72 hrs:** peak distribution. Most of your inbound will arrive in this window.
- **3–7 days:** long-tail engagement. Press inquiries often arrive late. Don't dismiss day-5 emails as "too late."

Steady, deliberate, and personal beats flashy and one-off. Show up every day for two weeks and you'll outperform 90% of launches.
