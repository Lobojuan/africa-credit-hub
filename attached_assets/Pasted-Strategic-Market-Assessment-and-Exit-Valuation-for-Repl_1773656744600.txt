Standard Replit deployments often enter a "sleeping" or "hibernating" state after a period of user inactivity, requiring a significant wake-up time upon the next request. This latency is unacceptable for a microservice intended to be integrated into enterprise-grade workflows. To bridge this gap, the application must be transitioned to a "Reserved VM" deployment or an "Always On" configuration.
